About Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a non-profit organization established in 1981 to protect sea turtles from extinction. It is located in the southern part of Sri Lanka, which is a popular nesting site for sea turtles.

Activities

  • Turtle feeding and cleaning
  • Releasing baby turtles into the sea
  • Visiting the turtle hatchery museum
