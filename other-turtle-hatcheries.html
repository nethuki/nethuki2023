Introduction

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is one of the most popular turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka. Located on the southern coast of the country, this hatchery has been operating for over two decades.

Turtle Conservation

The primary goal of Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is to conserve and protect the sea turtles that lay their eggs on the nearby beaches. The hatchery collects and incubates turtle eggs, and releases the hatchlings into the sea when they are ready.

Visiting the Hatchery

Tourists are welcome to visit Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery and learn about the conservation efforts taking place there. Visitors can see the hatchlings, learn about the different species of sea turtles, and even release hatchlings into the sea themselves.
