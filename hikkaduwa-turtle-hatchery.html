Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a conservation project that aims to protect sea turtles from extinction. Here, you can learn about the different types of turtles that inhabit the area, as well as their life cycle and habits. You can also participate in feeding the turtles and releasing the hatchlings into the sea.

Visit the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is open every day from 9 am to 6 pm. Admission is free, but donations are welcome to support the conservation efforts. You can also purchase souvenirs and other items to support the hatchery.
